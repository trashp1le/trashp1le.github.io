What is Devastation Baby?

Don't think Lit Mag. Think cassette label. Think a scrap of paper at the end of the world with a few beautiful words. Think of receiving an transmission from outerspace but it's just a coffee order, you know? We want to publish the pieces you are either too scared to send somewhere else or somewhere else is too scared to publish. We want your best and we want your worst but most of all we want to be devastated, whatever that might mean, baby.
