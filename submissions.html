Submissions

Devastation Baby is a literary magazine with a quarterly online edition and an annual print edition. Our first online volume will launch in January 2023 and we want your work to be a part of it!
We are currently open for submissions for fiction, creative nonfiction, poetry, visual arts, flash fiction, essays, and photography. We are looking for submissions from established, emerging, and never-before-published writers. While future editions will be themed, our first publication will be a collection of pieces we feel are definitive to Devastation Babyâ€™s direction.


GUIDELINES:
  • Please submit all work to info@devastationbaby.com with the type of piece and title of the work in the email subject line

  • Only submit original work that has not been previously published

  • Fiction and Nonfiction submissions should be under 5000 words, if your piece is longer than this please reach out with a pitch before submitting

  • Flash Fiction submissions should be between 100 and 1000 words

  • Poetry submissions should not exceed 10 pages total

  • No restrictions on visual arts or photography submissions although we may be limited in what we can accept by the constraints of format

  • Simultaneous submissions are absolutely fine, just let us know if your work is accepted elsewhere


OTHER THINGS YOU SHOULD KNOW:
Devastation Baby retains First Serial Rights. There is no cost to submit. We cannot pay contributors chosen for our online edition but if selected for our print edition our team will reach out about payment and further publishing rights.
