Submissions

Devastation Baby is a literary magazine with a triannual online edition and an annual print run of those editions.

We are now open for submissions for our second issue on Submittable! The theme for this issue will be "Past Regrets." We are open for fiction, creative nonfiction, poetry, flash fiction, and essays. We are looking for submissions from established, emerging, and never-before-published writers. Submissions will close June 15th.


GUIDELINES:
  • Only submit original work that has not been previously published

  • Fiction and Nonfiction submissions should be under 5000 words, if your piece is longer than this please reach out with a pitch before submitting

  • Flash Fiction submissions should be between 100 and 1000 words

  • Poetry submissions should not exceed 10 pages total

  • No restrictions on visual arts or photography submissions although we may be limited in what we can accept by the constraints of format

  • Simultaneous submissions are absolutely fine, just let us know if your work is accepted elsewhere


OTHER THINGS YOU SHOULD KNOW:
Devastation Baby retains First Serial Rights. There is no cost to submit. We are unable to pay contributors at this time but plan to do so for future editions. Please email any questions you may have to info@devastationbaby.com.
